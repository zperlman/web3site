The Discovery Phase

I understand that your business is very near and dear to your heart. We’ll start with a Discovery Call so I can learn more about you and your vision. I have carefully crafted questions designed to reveal the vision you have for your website. We’ll review these together, with the goal of further articulating your brand voice, honing in your business goals, and identifying your ideal customers.

The Design Phase

Now that I have a clear understanding of the direction of your business, I will bring your vision to life. I’ll provide you with preliminary designs to review and approve, and you’ll likely need to provide me with some elements such as text or photographs.

Coding and Testing

This is where things get real. The nuts and bolts of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript come together forming a functional set of web pages. We usually start with the home page, or the most important pages, gradually adding the less important ones. If you have a large e-commerce site or a lot of content, this step might take a few weeks.

Site Launch!

Time to share the magic with the rest of the world!

Growth & Maintenance

As time moves on there will be a million little ways to tweak things, ensuring that it continues to be a market-leading website. Routine site-speed optimizations are necessary and recurring SEO audits should be performed to ensure the site is viewed favorably by search engines and your site’s users.
